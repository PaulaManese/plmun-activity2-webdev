Belgium's capital, Brussels, is a delightful city with a fascinating history, a vibrant culture, and beautiful architecture.

The city is well known for its beautiful structures in the Gothic and Baroque styles, charming streets, and delectable gastronomy.

Brussels has a lot to offer travelers, from the famed Atomium to the renowned Manneken Pis statue.

The European Union and NATO headquarters are located in Brussels, making it a key hub for politics and diplomacy. With numerous global firms having their headquarters there, the city serves as a hub for international trade.

In addition to its importance in politics and industry, Brussels is renowned for its delectable cuisine, particularly its world-famous Belgian waffles, chocolate, and beer.

Guests can sample a variety of cuisines from around the world and benefit from the warm Belgian hospitality.
